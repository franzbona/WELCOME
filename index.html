Empower city businesses

Leverage on Crowds observation & measurement

We empower the urban ecosystem with awareness on city's dynamics (people flows and composition) offering a personalized crowd-analytics solution, enriched with comparisons based on information collected bottom-up from heterogeneous data sources, including crowdsourced data-creation from city actors.

Business activities can gain better knowledge (personalized views, impact assessment, predictions, ...) to improve their competitiveness and positioning, as well as the overall city appeal. Municipalities can benefit from reduced infrastructure costs and foster collaboration and data sharing between city's businesses.

OUR SERVICES

WELCOME comprises of a set of services which offer a complete overview of your city and its businesses. It extends the architecture of CrowdInsights, a solution which provides decision makers with reliable predictions and actionable insights on the composition, dynamics, and intentions of crowds in the urban environment.
